# Work Experience

## Principal Software Architect — Texture
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Dates:** Jun 2023 – Present
- **Location:** New York, NY (Hybrid)
- **Stage:** Founding engineer and first hire. Now a Series B company.

Texture is an AI-native platform for real-time energy operations coordination.
The company enables energy leaders to standardize data across assets, teams,
and partners while applying AI-driven logic to automate decisions and actions

and action by providing live data access, monitoring, and workflow automation

## Principal Software Architect — Extra Card
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Dates:** Jul 2022 – Jul 2023
- **Location:** New York, NY
- **Stage:** Joined during the seed stage. Extra was later acquired by Orchard.

Extra is a fintech company that offers a debit card designed to help users build
credit without a traditional credit card. Customers make purchases with their
Extra Debit Card, and the company spots them using available "Spend Power" (a
line of credit), automatically repays itself, and reports the transactions to
credit bureaus as creditworthy payments. The product targets people who either
cannot qualify for credit cards or prefer an alternative way to establish credit
history while earning rewards points.

## Engineering Lead — Knotel
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Dates:** Aug 2018 – Mar 2020
- **Location:** New York, NY
- **Stage:** Joined as the second engineering hire during the Series B stage. Helped grow the PDDE team to 30 people. Knotel was later acquired by Newmark in 2021.

Knotel was a flexible workspace platform that leased space from office landlords,
then designed, built, and managed customized offices on behalf of enterprise
clients. Unlike coworking spaces, Knotel exclusively served corporations with
short-term leases as a flexible alternative to traditional long-term office
leases. At its peak, Knotel operated in 250 buildings across 17 cities on 4
continents, with clients including DraftKings, Omnicom, and Twilio.
