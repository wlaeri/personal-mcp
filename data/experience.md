## Principal Software Architect — Extra
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Dates:** Jul 2022 – Jul 2023
- **Location:** Jupiter, FL (Remote)
- **Stage:** Acquired by Orchard in August 2023.

Extra is a fintech company that offers a debit card designed to help users build
credit without a traditional credit card. Customers make purchases with their
Extra Debit Card, and the company spots them using available "Spend Power" (a
line of credit), automatically repays itself, and reports the transactions to
credit bureaus as creditworthy payments. The product targets people who either
cannot qualify for credit cards or prefer an alternative way to establish credit
history while earning rewards points.
